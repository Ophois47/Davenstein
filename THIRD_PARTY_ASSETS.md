# Third-Party Assets

This document records known provenance and unresolved rights information for assets included with or depicted by Davenstein. It is not a license and does not grant permission to use third-party material.

## Wolfenstein 3D sprite material

The project maintainer reports that Wolfenstein 3D sprite material used by Davenstein was obtained from:

- The Spriters Resource: https://www.spriters-resource.com/ms_dos/wolfenstein3d/

The Spriters Resource states that original game assets remain the property of their respective owners and that the site cannot grant a license to use or redistribute those assets:

- https://www.spriters-resource.com/page/help/

The exact mapping between downloaded sprite sheets and processed files in this repository has not yet been documented.

Status: third-party copyrighted material; not covered by Davenstein's MIT or Apache 2.0 software licenses; express redistribution permission has not been established.

## Redistribution boundary

No third-party asset listed or referenced here is relicensed by Davenstein. A Davenstein software license applies only to the original software components described in `LICENSE.md`.

Before submitting Davenstein to an official package repository, application store, or commercial distribution channel, the project should either obtain the required written permissions, replace the affected material with original or appropriately licensed assets, or require users to supply lawfully obtained game data themselves.
